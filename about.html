From Humble Beginnings to Global Vision

Sarbaz Rajabi Co. was founded over 50 years ago, starting as a small store led by our father with a simple goal: to support Iranian agriculture. Today, we’ve grown into a trusted name in agricultural machinery imports, taking quality and customer satisfaction to the next level.

Our Mission

We aim to expand beyond borders and become a global leader in the agricultural equipment industry — bringing world-class machinery to farmers across Iran and, one day, around the world.
