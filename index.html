Welcome to Sarbaz Rajabi Co.

With over 50 years of trusted service, Sarbaz Rajabi Co. proudly stands as a leading importer of agricultural machinery in Iran. We bring quality products from China, Korea, and around the globe directly to Iranian farmers and agricultural businesses.

From humble beginnings in a small store founded by our father, we’ve grown into a modern enterprise serving thousands of clients who rely on durable, efficient equipment to run their operations.

Our mission is not only to deliver high-performance products, but also to build long-term partnerships. We are committed to expansion, innovation, and becoming a global player in the agricultural equipment market.

Why Choose Us?

  • Trusted Legacy: A half-century of experience in agricultural equipment
  • Global Network: Importing from top manufacturers worldwide
  • Customer-First Approach: Your satisfaction is our priority
  • Technical Support: Access to maintenance advice and service guidance
